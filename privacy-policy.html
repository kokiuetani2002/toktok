Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 4, 2026

1. Overview

TokTok ("the App") is a personal analytics tool that connects to TikTok via the official TikTok Display API to track video performance metrics for the account owner's own videos. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

2. Data We Collect

When you authenticate with TikTok, the App collects the following data from your TikTok account via the official API:

  • Basic profile information (display name, avatar)
  • Your own video list (video IDs, titles, creation times)
  • Performance metrics for your own videos (view count, like count, comment count, share count)

3. How We Use Your Data

All collected data is used solely to display personal analytics to the authenticated account owner. Data is stored in a private database accessible only to you. We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third party.

4. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as you use the App. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the TikTok Display API. Your use of TikTok is subject to TikTok's own Privacy Policy.
